Railway Station a key transportation hub in Ethiopia

  1. Home
  2. Railway station

Description

Dire Dawa Railway Station is a key transportation hub in Ethiopia, constructed in the early 20th century as part of the historic railway system linking the country to Djibouti. The station features an architectural blend of modern and traditional styles, reflecting its historical significance. It serves as the terminus for passenger trains and is vital for cargo transport to and from the port of Djibouti. The station is frequented by travelers and locals, contributing to the dynamic atmosphere of the area.
